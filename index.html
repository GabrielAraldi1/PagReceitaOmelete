Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total : Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation : 5 minutes
  • Cooking : 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-2 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Option filings: chesse, diced vegetable, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

1. Beat the eggs: a bowl, beat the eggs with salt and pepper until they are well mixed
2. heat the pan: heat the pan with oil or butter
3. Cook the omellete: Pour the eggs into the pan
4. Add fillings(optional): after about flipping to the other side of the pan, add the fillings you like
5. Fold and serve: not much to say about this step
6. Enjoy!: Serve hot and enjoy your meal
